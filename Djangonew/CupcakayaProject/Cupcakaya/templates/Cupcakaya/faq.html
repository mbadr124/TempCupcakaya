FAQs

What does Cupcakaya offer ?

Cupcakaya is a small local private business that specializes in custom homemade sweets; specifically cakes, cupcakes, cookies, and cake pops. You can choose from a variety of deisgns or even send in your own design.

Do you offer shipping ?

Yes, shipping is available everywhere in Egypt. The exact location and date are specified (in case of an occasion). We always try to ensure that the customer recieves the product the way that satisfies both parties.

Where did the name come from ?

The name "Cupcakaya" comes from a mix between the words cupcake and Aya, the name of the founder. In fact, the one who came up with the name was her younger sister, who was only 11 at the time.

What are the ingredients ?

All our products are homemade. We always try to use the best and freshest ingerdients weather talking about the batter's ingerdients or the toppings; and yes, IT TASTES AS GOOD AS IT LOOKS!
